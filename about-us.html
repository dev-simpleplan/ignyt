What Is SEO?

Search Engine Optimisation involves using techniques to amplify both the number and the calibre of visitors to your website through organic appearances in search engine results. 

More traffic to your site means more opportunities to grow your brand, turn casual browsers into committed customers, and, ultimately, boost your bottom line. SEO has evolved considerably over time and continues to do so.

Why?

To keep up with changes in search engine algorithms, user behaviour, and technological advancements. So you can’t just implement basic SEO tactics, sit back and hope for the best!

You need to follow Google’s E.E.A.T. principle:

  1. Experience
  2. Expertise
  3. Authoritativeness
  4. Trustworthiness

Incorporating these key factors in your niche is essential because Google uses them to evaluate and rank the quality of content on a webpage.

Google and other search engine results pages don’t just show websites with text; they show information on websites and social media like YouTube and more. This information is delivered via text, pictures, graphics, and videos.
